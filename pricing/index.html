Common Questions

Do you retain the images?

No. Locy is a zero-retention pipeline. Images are processed in volatile memory and discarded immediately after coordinate extraction. We do not use your data to train our models.

What counts as a 'Look'?

A 'Look' is any successful service request or WhatsApp message that returns a valid coordinate or route. Errors and ambiguous results are not billed.

Is there a volume limit?

The Standard tier supports up to 10 requests per second (RPS). For higher throughput, please contact Enterprise sales for dedicated instances.

Can I cache the coordinates?

Yes. Once you pay for the data, it is yours. You are free to cache, store, and reuse the coordinates indefinitely.
